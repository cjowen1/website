Science News

  • 03/2025: New preprint: we reveal the proton slingshot effect in solid-acid electrolyte materials using machine learned force fields. This challenges long-held assumptions of the mechanism and allows for the development of design rules for increasing the conductivity of these systems.
  • 07/2024: New preprint: we challenge the assumed surface structure of nanocatalysts using machine learned force fields. Such computational methods are proven to be capable of decoupling disparate experimental observables, allowing for more direct understanding of such dynamic phenomena.
